Military Car Shipping with AmeriFreight: What to Know

Introduction to Military Car Shipping

For military personnel, frequent relocations and PCS (Permanent Change of Station) moves are a part of life. AmeriFreight understands the unique challenges faced by military families and offers reliable, affordable, and stress-free car shipping services tailored to their needs.

Learn more about AmeriFreight’s military discounts and services designed specifically for military personnel.

AmeriFreight is a trusted name in the car shipping industry, offering exclusive benefits for military families, including:

  • Military Discounts: Special pricing for active-duty service members, veterans, and their families.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Accommodates tight timelines and last-minute PCS orders.
  • Reliable Service: Partners with licensed and insured carriers to ensure your vehicle’s safety during transit.

Steps to Ship Your Car with AmeriFreight

1. Get a Quote

Start by using AmeriFreight’s quote calculator to get an instant estimate for your car shipping needs.

2. Choose the Right Transport Option

Decide between open auto transport for affordability or enclosed auto transport for added protection, depending on your vehicle type and budget.

3. Schedule Your Shipment

Book your car shipment in advance to ensure availability and avoid last-minute stress. AmeriFreight’s team will work with you to find the best schedule for your PCS move.

4. Prepare Your Vehicle

Clean your car, remove personal belongings, and document its condition with photos before shipping. AmeriFreight provides a detailed guide on how to prepare your car for shipping.

5. Track Your Shipment

AmeriFreight keeps you informed throughout the shipping process, providing updates on your vehicle’s status and ensuring peace of mind during your PCS move.
